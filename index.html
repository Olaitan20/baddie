Welcome

We are a diverse group of friendly badminton fans based in the borough of Barnet North London. Everyone is welcome regardless of abilities. We have novice, intermediate and advanced players and provide coaching and guidance.

At Badminton Excel, we develop your hidden skills, stamina and fitness. We have been successful in using badminton as one of the tools for a healthy weight loss programme in line with our lifestyle coach. You can play as intensely as you wish or as moderately as you desire. Periodic fitness assessments are conducted to help manage fitness levels.
